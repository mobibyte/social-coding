Mission Statement

OurScene, LLC. is dedicated to revolutionizing the live music industry by creating a dynamic platform that connects local bands with promoters, venues, and enthusiastic fans. Our mission is to empower emerging artists, boost the local music scene, and enrich the lives of music enthusiasts

Company Overview

OurScene, LLC. was founded by Bryan Cox, passionate music enthusiasts with extensive experience in event management and technology development. Headquartered in Dallas, Texas, OurScene aims to bridge the gap between talented local bands and the vibrant music ecosystem in the region.

Market Opportunity

The live music industry in Dallas, Texas, and beyond presents a significant opportunity for growth and innovation. Local bands often struggle to find suitable gigs and reach their target audience, while promoters and venues face challenges in discovering emerging talent. OurScene addresses these needs in a market expected to exceed 1.5 billion dollars by 2025.
